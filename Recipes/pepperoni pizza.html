Pizza Recipe

User:Apalapala, Pepperoni pizza, CC BY-SA 3.0

Description:

Making a delicious pepperoni pizza at home is simple and can be done in under 30 minutes if you use a quick dough method as you will see next.

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough: 1 lb of store-bought or homemade dough.
  • Pizza sauce: 1/2 to 1 cup of tomato or pizza sauce.
  • Cheese: 2 cups (approx. 8 oz) of shredded mozzarella cheese.
  • Pepperoni: 1/2 to 1 cup of sliced pepperoni.
  • Optional seasoning: Dried oregano, garlic powder, or red pepper flakes for extra flavor.

Preparation instructions:

  1. Set your oven to 475°F (245°C). If you have a pizza stone, place it inside while the oven heats for at least 15-20 minutes to ensure a crispy crust.
  2. On a lightly floured surface, stretch or roll the dough into a 12-inch circle. Transfer it to a parchment-lined baking sheet or a pizza pan.
  3. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a 1/2-inch border for the crust. Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese generously over the sauce.
  4. Arrange the pepperoni slices in a single layer over the cheese. For extra flavor, sprinkle some oregano or garlic powder on top.
  5. Slide the pizza into the oven and bake for 10 to 15 minutes. It is ready when the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbling and slightly browned.
  6. Let the pizza rest for 3 to 5 minutes before slicing to allow the cheese to set.
